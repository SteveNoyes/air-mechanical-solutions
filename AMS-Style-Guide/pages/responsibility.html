Responsibility to our community and our society in an ever-complex world.

Serving the people around us is as important as serving our government customers. That’s why we take our responsibilities as seriously as we would any mission. At Trace, we make sure our collective impact on the community is as positive as possible.

From individual volunteering to corporate sponsored contributions and events, we strive to give back to the communities where we live, work and play. Our initiatives focus on providing support to retired and active duty military, STEM education to prepare for future threats, and health and wellness.
